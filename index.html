How Our AI Works

Our intelligent AI engine performs a deep, automated security audit. Here's the process:

  • Link Analysis

    We start with your website's URL and automatically discover all internal links, forms, and entry points.

  • Page‑by‑Page Exploration

    The AI visits every page, clicks every button, and tests every interactive element just like a real user.

  • Vulnerability Detection

    We simulate real‑world attacks to uncover SQL injection, XSS, misconfigurations, and other security flaws.

  • Smart Reporting

    All findings are prioritised, with step‑by‑step fixes tailored to your selected plan.

You will receive your detailed report in HTML format directly on your profile page within 24 hours.
